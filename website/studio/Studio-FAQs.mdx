<details>

<summary> How many InVEST models are we supposed to use for the project? </summary>

You are to choose at least 1 model to use in your analysis that is relevant to the topic your team chose.

</details>

<details>

<summary> How long is each presentation? </summary>

Each team presentation is 10 minutes followed by a 10 minutes Q&A/feedback from studio leader. 

</details>

<details>

<summary> Are all team members supposed to present for each presentation? </summary>

Each team member have to present at least once out of the three studios. However, if everyone in the team would like to present for each presentation studio, you may do so too. 

</details>
