:::About the Presentation

In this studio, your team and yourself are required to present your preliminary solution(s) guided by InVEST. You should discuss how the proposed preliminary solution(s) could solve the problem(s) identified, and the various benefit(s) and/or tradeoff(s) that the solution(s) might have. 

Presentation is 10 minutes for each team, followed by 10 minutes of feedback. 

:::
